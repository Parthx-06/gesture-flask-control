About GestureFlow

AI-powered hand gesture recognition to control your entire PC — no touch required.

🧠

AI Hand Tracking

Uses Google's MediaPipe for real-time hand landmark detection with sub-frame latency, recognizing 8+ distinct gestures.

🖱️

Full PC Control

Move the cursor, click, double-click, scroll, adjust volume, switch windows, take screenshots — all with your hands.

🌐

Web Dashboard

Live video stream, real-time gesture status, adjustable settings, and a screenshot gallery — all in your browser.

How It Works

Camera Captures Your Hand

OpenCV reads frames from your webcam at high resolution for precise tracking.

MediaPipe Detects Landmarks

Google's AI model identifies 21 hand landmarks in real-time, determining finger positions.

Gesture Recognition

Finger states are analyzed to recognize gestures — one finger, pinch, fist, open palm, and more.

System Action Executed

The recognized gesture triggers the corresponding action — cursor movement, clicks, volume control, etc.
